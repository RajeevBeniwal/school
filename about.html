🏫 School Overview

Govt. Girls High School Kagdana is a government-run institution located in the rural village of Kagdana, Sirsa District of Haryana. The school operates under the Haryana School Education Department and is committed to providing quality education exclusively to girls.


The school offers classes from Class VI to Class X following the Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE) curriculum. It provides free and compulsory education under the Right to Education Act.


  • Affiliated with: Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE), Bhiwani
  • Medium of Instruction: Hindi & English
  • Location: Village Kagdana, Sirsa
  • Working Hours: 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Mon–Sat)

🎯 Vision

To be a premier educational institution that empowers every girl with knowledge, skills, values, and confidence to excel in life and contribute positively to society and the nation.

📌 Mission

  • Provide quality education to every girl regardless of social background
  • Create a safe, inclusive and motivating learning environment
  • Promote academic excellence alongside character development
  • Bridge the gender gap in education in rural Haryana
  • Equip students with skills for the 21st century

📅 Historical Timeline


1985
School established as Govt Girls Middle School, Kagdana
1994
Upgraded to High School level; Classes IX & X introduced
2002
New building constructed; library and science lab added
2010
Mid-day meal programme and scholarship schemes launched
2018
Computer lab established under Digital India initiative
2024
School website launched for digital connectivity with students and parents

📋 Admission Information

Eligibility

  • Girls only (Class VI to X)
  • Age appropriate to class level
  • Must have passed previous class
  • Priority to village and nearby area students

Documents Required

  • Previous school Transfer Certificate
  • Mark sheet of last class
  • Aadhar Card copy
  • Passport size photographs (2)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
📅 Admission Period: April – June every year. Contact school office for current year dates.
