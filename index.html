About Our School

Govt. Girls High School Kagdana is a government institution located in Village Kagdana, Sirsa District, Haryana. Established in 1985, the school has served the community for over three decades providing quality education to girls from Classes VI to X.

The school is committed to providing a holistic education that balances academic rigour with cultural values, sports and extra-curricular activities, all in a safe and nurturing environment.
